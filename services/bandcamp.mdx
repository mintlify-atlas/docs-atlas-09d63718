Bandcamp is an independent music distribution platform supported by NewPipe Extractor, focusing on artist-direct music sales and streaming.

## Media Capabilities

  <Card title="Audio Streams" icon="music">
    Stream audio tracks from independent artists
  </Card>

  </Card>
  <Card title="No Video" icon="video-slash">

## Supported Features

### Content Types

- **Tracks**: Individual songs with full metadata
- **Albums**: Full album releases with multiple tracks
- **EPs**: Extended play releases
- **Singles**: Single track releases
- **Compilations**: Curated track collections

### Artists and Labels

- Artist profile pages
- Label pages with catalog listings
- Discography browsing
- Artist descriptions and information
- Channel tabs for content organization

### Playlists and Albums

- Album track listings
- Artist discographies
- Label catalogs
- Track ordering and metadata

### Search

- Search across tracks, albums, and artists
- Search suggestions for discovery
- Content filtering by type

### Kiosks (Discovery)

Bandcamp offers curated discovery feeds:

- **Featured**: Editorial picks and featured releases (default kiosk)
- **Radio**: Bandcamp Radio shows and episodes

<Info>
Bandcamp's featured content showcases hand-picked releases from the editorial team, providing quality music discovery.
</Info>

## Special Features

### Bandcamp Radio

The service includes special support for Bandcamp Radio:

- Radio show episodes and archives
- Curated music selections
- Special radio stream extraction via `BandcampRadioStreamExtractor`

<Note>
Radio URLs are detected automatically and use a specialized extractor for proper handling.
</Note>

### Comments System

Bandcamp's fan-focused comment system:

- Fan reviews and feedback
- Artist responses to fans
- Community discussions on releases

### Track Metadata

- High-quality audio information
- Lyrics (when provided by artists)
- Credits and liner notes
- Release dates and catalog numbers
- Pricing and purchase information

## Technical Details

**Base URL**: `https://bandcamp.com`

**Service ID**: 4

**Link Handler Factories**:
- Stream: `BandcampStreamLinkHandlerFactory`
- Channel: `BandcampChannelLinkHandlerFactory`
- Playlist: `BandcampPlaylistLinkHandlerFactory`
- Comments: `BandcampCommentsLinkHandlerFactory`
- Featured: `BandcampFeaturedLinkHandlerFactory`

**Special Extractors**:
- Regular tracks: `BandcampStreamExtractor`
- Radio streams: `BandcampRadioStreamExtractor`

## Limitations

<Note>
Bandcamp support has certain limitations:

- No video content support (audio-only platform)
- No live streaming capabilities
- Subscription extraction is not available
- Some premium or paid content may have restricted access
- Download availability depends on artist settings
</Note>

## Platform Features

### Artist-First Platform

Bandcamp's unique features reflected in the extractor:

- Direct artist support through sales
- "Name your price" releases
- Free streaming with optional purchases
- Artist-controlled release dates

### Discovery Tools

- Genre and tag-based browsing
- Featured content curation
- Radio show recommendations
- Related artist suggestions
