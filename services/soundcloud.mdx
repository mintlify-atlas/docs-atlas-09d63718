SoundCloud is an audio-focused streaming platform supported by NewPipe Extractor, providing access to music tracks, podcasts, and user-generated audio content.

## Supported Features

### Content Types

- **Tracks**: Individual audio tracks with full metadata
- **Albums and EPs**: Collections of tracks
- **Podcasts**: Episodic audio content
- **Remixes and DJ Sets**: User-uploaded mixes

### Users and Channels

- User/artist profiles and information
- Channel tabs for organizing content
- Upload history and track lists
- Follower and following counts

### Playlists

- User-created playlists
- Album playlists
- Playlist metadata and descriptions
- Track ordering and durations

### Search

- Full-text search across tracks, users, and playlists
- Search suggestions and autocomplete
- Content type filtering (tracks, users, playlists)

### Kiosks (Charts)

SoundCloud provides trending content through its charts system:

- **New & Hot**: Trending and newly released tracks (default kiosk)

<Info>
The "New & Hot" kiosk showcases trending music and is set as the default discovery endpoint.
</Info>

## Country Support

SoundCloud charts are available for specific countries:

- **AU**: Australia
- **CA**: Canada
- **DE**: Germany
- **FR**: France
- **GB**: United Kingdom
- **IE**: Ireland
- **NL**: Netherlands
- **NZ**: New Zealand
- **US**: United States

<Note>
Chart content varies by country, reflecting regional music trends and preferences.
</Note>

## Special Features

### Comments System

SoundCloud's unique timed comment system is supported:

- Comments can be linked to specific timestamps in tracks
- Read comment threads and user discussions
- View comment counts and engagement metrics

### Track Information

- Waveform data visualization
- Play counts and likes
- Track descriptions and tags
- Related tracks and recommendations

### Subscription Support

The service includes subscription extraction, allowing users to export and manage their followed artists and channels.

## Technical Details

**Base URL**: `https://soundcloud.com`

**Service ID**: 1

**Link Handler Factories**:
- Stream: `SoundcloudStreamLinkHandlerFactory`
- Channel: `SoundcloudChannelLinkHandlerFactory`
- Playlist: `SoundcloudPlaylistLinkHandlerFactory`
- Comments: `SoundcloudCommentsLinkHandlerFactory`
- Charts: `SoundcloudChartsLinkHandlerFactory`

## Limitations

<Note>
SoundCloud support has some limitations:

- No video content support (audio-only platform)
- No live streaming capabilities
- Some tracks may be region-locked or require SoundCloud Go
- Private tracks and content require authentication
- Download options depend on track settings
</Note>
